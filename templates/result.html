Some tips to prevent Breast Cancer

Eat healthy foods

Eat a variety of healthy foods, including fruits, vegetables, lean proteins, and whole grains. Limit foods high in saturated fat, transfat, added sugars, salt and processed foods.

Be Cautious with Birth Control Pills

Long-term use of birth control pills may slightly increase breast cancer risk, so consult with a healthcare provider about alternatives.

Avoid or Limit HRT

(Hormone Replacement Therapy)
Long-term use of combined HRT(estrogen and progesterone) has been linked to an increased risk of breast cancer. The risk appears to increase significantly when taken for more than five years.

Avoid Smoking & Limit Alcohol

Smoking is a known cause of many cancers, including breast cancer, especially in premenopausal women.Even small amounts of alcohol can increase breast cancer risk.

Breastfeed, If possible

Research shows that women who breastfeed have a lower risk of developing breast cancer. The protective effect is stronger the longer you breastfeed.
